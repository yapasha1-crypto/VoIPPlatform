Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 30, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information necessary to provide our telecommunications services:

  • Account Information: Name, email, company details, billing address
  • Usage Data: Call records, SMS logs, API requests (metadata only)
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, device information, browser type
  • Payment Information: Processed securely via PCI-DSS compliant providers

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Providing and maintaining our VoIP and SMS services
  • Processing payments and generating invoices
  • Sending service notifications and support communications
  • Improving our platform and developing new features
  • Complying with legal obligations and preventing fraud

3. Data Sharing

We never sell your personal information. We only share data with:

  • Carrier Partners: To complete calls and deliver messages
  • Payment Processors: To process transactions securely
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law or to protect rights

4. Data Retention

Call and SMS metadata is retained for 90 days for billing and support purposes. Account information is kept while your account is active and for 7 years after closure for legal compliance.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request corrections or deletions
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Security & Compliance

Security is at the core of everything we do. We employ industry-leading practices to protect your data and communications.

Encryption

  • TLS 1.3 for all API traffic
  • SRTP for voice encryption
  • AES-256 for data at rest

Certifications

  • SOC 2 Type II Certified
  • ISO 27001 Compliant
  • PCI-DSS Level 1

Infrastructure

  • Multi-region redundancy
  • DDoS protection
  • 24/7 security monitoring

Vulnerability Disclosure

We welcome responsible disclosure of security vulnerabilities. If you discover a security issue, please email security@beroea.com with details. We commit to acknowledging reports within 24 hours.

Incident Response

In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected customers within 72 hours and provide detailed information about the incident and remediation steps.

Regular Audits

Our systems undergo quarterly security audits by independent third-party firms. Penetration testing is performed annually to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.
